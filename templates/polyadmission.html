Diploma Polytechnic

Polytechnic is a diploma-level technical education program designed for students who have completed their 10th grade. It offers practical and job-oriented training in various fields such as Information Technology, Computer Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and more. The duration of the course is typically 3 years, and it equips students with the skills and knowledge needed to start a professional career or pursue higher education like B.E./B.Tech.

Eligibility

Students who have passed 10th standard (SSC) from a recognized board with required marks are eligible for admission.

Duration

Polytechnic diploma courses typically span 3 years, offering focused technical training after Class 10.

Programs

Offered in streams like Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Mechanical, Civil, Electronics, and more.

Common Documents

  • SSC Mark Sheet
  • Personal Email and Mobile Number
  • Password Photo + Sign
  • Technical / Intermediate Drawing Certificate (if have)
  • Aadhar Card
  • Nationality and Domicile Certificate
  • HSC Mark Sheet (Direct Second Year Polytechnic DSD)
  • Leaving / Transfer Certificate (TC) / Proforma N
  • Income Certificate

Category-Specific Documents

  • Caste Certificate (All Caste) (SC, ST, VJ, OBC, SBC, SEBC)
  • Non-Creamy Layer Certificate
  • EWS Certificate Issued By Govt Of Maharashtra
  • Income Certificate
