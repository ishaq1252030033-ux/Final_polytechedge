Direct Second Year Engineering

Direct Second Year Engineering (DSE) is a unique opportunity for diploma holders to directly enter the second year of B.E./B.Tech programs. This pathway saves one year of study while maintaining the quality of education.

Eligibility

Diploma holders in Engineering/Technology with minimum 50% marks

Duration

3 years (instead of 4 years) to complete B.E./B.Tech

Programs

Available in various branches like Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, etc.

Required Documents

Common Documents

  • Diploma Final Year Memo
  • Aadhar Card
  • SSC Mark Sheet
  • HSC Mark Sheet (if required)
  • Leaving / Transfer Certificate (diploma TC)
  • Nationality and Domicile Certificate
  • Income Certificate
  • Gap Certificate (if required)
  • Personal Email and Mobile Number
  • Password Photo + Sign

Category-Specific Documents

  • Caste Certificate (All Caste) (SC, ST, VJ, OBC, SBC, SEBC)
  • Caste Validity (All Caste)
  • Non-Creamy Layer Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Validity Certificate
  • DEFENSE 1 (3%)- EX-SER Certificate Proforma C + Domicile Certificate of Maharashtra
  • DEFENSE 2 (1%)- ACTIVE-SER Certificate Proforma C + Domicile Certificate of Defense Person
  • DEFENSE 3 (1%)- ACTIVE-SER Certificate Proforma C + Proforma D/E + Transfer Cert of Defense Person
  • Disputed Area Certificate Proforma G1
  • Mother Tongue Certificate Proforma G2
  • Domicile Certificate of Candidate
  • PWD Certificate
  • Proforma F/F-1
  • EWS Certificate Issued By Govt Of Maharashtra - Proforma V
  • Orphan Certificate As Per Proforma - U
  • Proforma in format J, K, L
  • Declaration Of Minority or Certificate Mentioning Minority Religious or Lingustic
